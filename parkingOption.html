Parking

Parking is allowed for long durations in the parking garrage as well as reservation parking from outside vendors. Parking is allowed for short durations (under 1 hr) in the cell phone lot.

Parking Garage Access

The Parking garage is accessible to anyone. You will get a ticket at the gate and will enter and park. This will cost you $5 per hour of your stay. To access this parking garage follow signs to the arrivals and departures then make the first left to follow signs to "Parking". There is also temporary cell phone lot parking for up to an hour.
